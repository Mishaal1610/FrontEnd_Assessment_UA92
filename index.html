FAQs

At SpeakStride, we offer a wide range of languages including English, French, German, Mandarin, Arabic, Spanish, and many more. Our courses are designed to cater to learners at all levels, from beginners to advanced speakers..

Our courses are structured into comprehensive modules that cover essential language skills such as listening, speaking, reading, and writing. Each module includes interactive lessons, practice exercises, and assessments to help reinforce your learning. You’ll also have access to additional resources like video tutorials, flashcards, and conversational practice sessions.

We offer a variety of support options to ensure you have the best learning experience. You can reach out to our instructors via email or through our online platform’s messaging system. Additionally, we have a vibrant community forum where you can ask questions, share experiences, and get advice from fellow learners and instructors. For technical issues, our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you.
